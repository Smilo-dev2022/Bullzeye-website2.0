Our partnership with M‑KOPA

M‑KOPA is a technology and financial platform that enables customers to access products through flexible, pay‑as‑you‑go financing. In South Africa, M‑KOPA helps people own essential devices and services affordably.

Through our collaboration with M‑KOPA South Africa, eligible customers can access flexible payment options on select security packages and equipment. This makes professional security more accessible for households and small businesses.
